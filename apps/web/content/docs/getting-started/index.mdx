---
title: Getting started
description: Connect SeoGrep to your AI client and run your first analysis.
---

Getting started takes three steps: get your personal MCP URL, add it to your AI
client, and ask for an analysis in plain language.

1. **Get your MCP URL.** From your SeoGrep dashboard, copy your personal MCP
   endpoint. In these docs it appears as `YOUR_MCP_URL`.
2. **Add it to your client.** Paste `YOUR_MCP_URL` into the MCP server settings
   of the client you already use — Claude Desktop, claude.ai, Claude Code,
   Cursor, or Windsurf.
3. **Ask in plain language.** Try "Crawl my site and find quick wins." Credits
   pay for the analysis work; your AI subscription stays your own.

> **Beta note:** SeoGrep is in private beta. Setup details and the exact tool
> list will be finalized at launch.
