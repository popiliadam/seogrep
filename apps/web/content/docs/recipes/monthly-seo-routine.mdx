This is the `monthly-routine` prompt: a repeatable monthly check that
compares fresh Search Console data against your last crawl.

> **Beta note:** The exact cadence and tool set may still change before
> launch.

## Goal

Catch decay and new opportunities every month without re-running a full
audit.

## Example dialog

1. **You:** "Run my monthly SEO check for example.com."
2. **Ranklens:** runs `pull_gsc_data`, then `analyze_content_decay` and
   `find_quick_wins` against the latest numbers.
3. **You:** "What should I do first?"
   **Ranklens:** runs `whats_next` to point at the highest-impact item, then
   offers `generate_report` for a shareable summary.

## What you get

A short list of pages losing clicks, a short list of easy ranking wins, and
a suggested next action.
