**Cost:** Free (0 credits).

`get_credit_balance` reports your available credits — the running total of your credit ledger.

## What it does

Sums your credit ledger, scoped to your account, and returns the available balance. Paid tools debit credits when they run; a balance of 0 blocks paid tools until you top up.

## Example

Ask your MCP client in plain language:

> How many credits do I have left?

### Input

No parameters.

### Returns

Your available credit balance.
