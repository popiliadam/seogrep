---
title: Troubleshooting
description: Fixes for the most common SeoGrep connection issues in beta.
---

Most setup problems come down to three things: a mistyped URL, a client
that needs a restart, or a beta limit. Check these before anything else.

> **Beta note:** Beta limits and error messages may change before launch.

## SeoGrep tools don't show up in chat

- **Symptom:** you added the connector, but no SeoGrep tools appear.
- **Check:** open your client's MCP or connector settings and confirm the
  URL matches `YOUR_MCP_URL` exactly, with no trailing spaces or line
  breaks.
- **Fix:** remove and re-add the connector, then start a brand-new chat —
  most clients only refresh their tool list on a new session.

## "Connection failed" or similar error

- **Symptom:** the client shows an error when you add the URL.
- **Check:** confirm you pasted the full URL with no missing characters,
  trailing spaces, or line breaks — partial pastes are a common cause.
- **Fix:** your MCP URL is only ever shown once, right when a key is
  created or rotated — the dashboard shows just a masked prefix after
  that. If you no longer have the original copy, rotate your key from the
  Connection page: it mints a fresh URL on the spot (the old URL stops
  working immediately) so you can copy the new one and paste it fresh.

## Tools worked, then stopped

- **Symptom:** SeoGrep tools were working and then disappeared.
- **Check:** restart the client — some clients drop remote connectors after
  being idle or after an update.
- **Fix:** if restarting doesn't help, re-add the connector. If you no
  longer have your original MCP URL saved, rotate your key from the
  Connection page to get a new one — the old URL stops working as soon as
  you do.

## Runs stop with an out-of-credits message

- **Symptom:** a run stops and the reply says you're out of credits.
- **Check:** ask `get_credit_balance` in your chat to see your remaining
  balance.
- **Fix:** trial credits are a one-time grant during beta; top-ups and
  plans open at launch — see [pricing](/pricing).

## Still stuck

We're in private beta, so rough edges are expected. Email support@seogrep.com and describe what you tried — see
[FAQ](/docs/faq) for more common questions.
