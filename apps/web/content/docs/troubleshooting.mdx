---
title: Troubleshooting
description: Fixes for the most common Ranklens connection issues in beta.
---

Most setup problems come down to three things: a mistyped URL, a client
that needs a restart, or a beta limit. Check these before anything else.

> **Beta note:** Beta limits and error messages may change before launch.

## Ranklens tools don't show up in chat

- **Symptom:** you added the connector, but no Ranklens tools appear.
- **Check:** open your client's MCP or connector settings and confirm the
  URL matches `YOUR_MCP_URL` exactly, with no trailing spaces or line
  breaks.
- **Fix:** remove and re-add the connector, then start a brand-new chat —
  most clients only refresh their tool list on a new session.

## "Connection failed" or similar error

- **Symptom:** the client shows an error when you add the URL.
- **Check:** confirm you pasted the full URL exactly as your dashboard
  shows it — partial pastes are a common cause.
- **Fix:** copy the URL again from the dashboard and paste it fresh, rather
  than editing the old one.

## Tools worked, then stopped

- **Symptom:** Ranklens tools were working and then disappeared.
- **Check:** restart the client — some clients drop remote connectors after
  being idle or after an update.
- **Fix:** if restarting doesn't help, re-add the connector with a freshly
  copied URL from the dashboard.

## Runs stop with an out-of-credits message

- **Symptom:** a run stops and the reply says you're out of credits.
- **Check:** ask `get_credit_balance` in your chat to see your remaining
  balance.
- **Fix:** trial credits are a one-time grant during beta; top-ups and
  plans open at launch — see [pricing](/pricing).

## Still stuck

We're in private beta, so rough edges are expected. Reach us via the
waitlist email you used and describe what you tried — see
[FAQ](/docs/faq) for more common questions.
