title: Data retention
description: What Ranklens keeps, for how long, and how to request deletion.
---

Ranklens keeps different data for different lengths of time, and deleting
your account removes all of it.

> **Beta note:** Retention windows below are the current beta policy and may
> be refined before launch.

## What's kept, and for how long

- **Crawl raw data** — kept for 90 days, then dropped automatically.
- **Report output** — kept for the lifetime of your account, so you can
  reference or reshare it later.

## Deleting your data

A deletion request is a full purge — projects, crawl history, reports, and
any connected GSC tokens are all removed, not just hidden. There's no
partial "forget this site" option in beta.
