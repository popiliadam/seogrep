SeoGrep keeps different data for different lengths of time. To close your
account and have your data removed, contact us and we will handle it for
you.

> **Beta note:** Retention windows below are the current beta policy and may
> be refined before launch.

## What's kept, and for how long

- **Crawl raw data** — during beta, retained while your account is active.
- **Search Console data** — during beta, retained while your account is
  active.
- **Report output** — kept for the lifetime of your account, so you can
  reference or reshare it later.

## Deleting your data

To have your data deleted, email us and we will remove it. There's no
self-serve delete option in beta yet, so reach out and we'll handle it for
you.
