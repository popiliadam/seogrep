---

Ranklens runs on monthly credit plans with optional top-ups — see
[/pricing](/pricing) for the current draft numbers. This page covers billing
mechanics, not the prices themselves.

> **Beta note:** Billing goes live at launch; beta accounts run on trial
> credits only.

## Plans and top-ups

Paid plans renew your credit balance every month. If you run out mid-month,
a top-up adds credits without changing your plan. Unused monthly credits
roll over for one month, capped at twice your plan's monthly amount.

## Payment provider

Payments are handled by Paddle, our merchant of record, so receipts, tax,
and card handling go through their checkout — Ranklens never stores your
card details.

## Checking usage

Ask "What's my credit balance?" to run `get_credit_balance` any time, or
open the dashboard's Usage tab for a full ledger of what each run cost.

## Changing or cancelling

Plan changes and cancellations happen from the dashboard's Billing tab,
which opens Paddle's billing portal.
